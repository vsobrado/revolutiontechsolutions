About Us

 

 

Revolution Technology Solutions specializes in streamling technology transitions for businesses of all sizes in the greater Denver area.

 

 

Today's office environments are far less about paper and filing cabinets, and more about technology and data than ever before. Workstations and servers are at the very heart of a modern office.

Office transitions don't have to cause significant downtime for your employees – that's why it makes sense to have an experienced team inventory and connect all your workstations, servers and peripheral devices in a short period of time when it's most convenient for you.

At Revolution Technology Solutions we recognize that every office has unique needs – our team will work closely with your management team to get your business up and running in a timely manner.

Whether you’re looking for someone to handle disconnect/reconnect services for several workstations or hundreds of workstations for your office's next big move, we’re here to help.

 

We are committed to providing excellent customer service to our clients and we value our great reputation with prior clients in the greater Denver area.
